About WCWD

WCWD is a practical, text-first resource for exploring the World ecosystem with a focus on builder-friendly references and lightweight tools. The site collects notes, links, and simple utilities for World Chain and World ID flows. It is maintained as a best-effort project and will continue to evolve as the ecosystem changes.
