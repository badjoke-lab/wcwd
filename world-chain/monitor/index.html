World Chain Monitor

Detailed server-owned summary view for World Chain health, WLD market data, activity, stored history, alerts, events, and verified daily compacts.

History is read from existing bounded KV snapshots. Automatic collection is disabled. Estimates, observations, and unavailable values are labeled separately.
